ABOUT US:

Founded in 1999 by SUGEERTH, NAGAKARTHICK, PARSAMSHARATH AND SRIKANTH.FRIENDS & CO. was created to offer revolutionary web solutions and online marketing services to growing and midsized business. During the first few years, the company made a gradual shift from being a local coimbatore based company to a company with global reach, taking on larger clients as the company itself has grown.

 

OUR MODEL:

The web solutions and online marketing services are offered in our Web Department model. This model has been successfully put to work through a global portfolio of clients across all COUNTRIES. The company takes great pride in the multiple patent pending software it has built, as well as their award winning e-commerce sites.FRIENDS & CO. has evolved from being a local e-commerce solution provider to a well respected, full-service web solutions firm.

Headquartered in Coimbatore,INDIA Web Solutions also has a dedicated team in Chennai, India, registered as friends & co., Pvt. Ltd.
